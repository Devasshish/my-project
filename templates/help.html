Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.
How can I change my profile picture?
To change your profile picture, go to your profile settings. Click on the "Edit Profile" button and you'll see an option to upload a new profile picture.
How do I report inappropriate content?
If you come across inappropriate content, click on the three dots (...) next to the post and select "Report". Choose the reason for reporting and submit your report.
